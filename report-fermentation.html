The Physical Rate-Limiting Steps

Hydrostatic Pressure

The Reality

In a massive vertical bioreactor, the sheer height of the fluid creates immense pressure at the bottom. This hydrostatic gradient alters cellular metabolism, mutating the very organisms you are trying to cultivate.

Mechanical Shear Stress

The Reality

To achieve necessary oxygen transfer in a 50,000L vertical tank, impellers must spin at lethal speeds. This mechanical shear physically shreds fragile cell cultures, plummeting overall yield and destroying product margins.
